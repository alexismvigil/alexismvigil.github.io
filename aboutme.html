About Me

Hi friends! My name is Alexis Vigil, I also go by Lexi, I'm a 21 year old full-time student at Colorado State University-Pueblo. I am a Mass Communications major with an emphasis in integrated communication and a minor in health promotions. I love helping people and work part-time as a nursing assistant.

I'm very girly and love all things pink and glittery. I love social media and could see myself doing something with that. My interests are in fashion, beauty, lifestyle, and wellness. I also like to read, write, and do anything creative. My hobbies include but are not limited to yoga, snowboarding, baking, and traveling.

I'm not exactly sure what I want to do when I "grow up" but I'm really happy with the direction I'm headed and hope to graduate in 2021.

I was born in Salt Lake City, Utah and grew up in a small suburb about 30 miles west called Tooele. I am two years older than my younger brother Michael. We have a dog named Deja vu who is the sweetest yellow lab you would ever meet. I don't know about you but I have always considered pets to be more like family! I am totally a dog person and aspire to have a dog farm with lots of dogs!

We loved our life in Utah but in Summer of 2013 we made a move from our longtime home onto a new adventure in Colorado which we now also love! I finished high school at Pueblo County. I was a very active high school student usually always on the honor roll and played soccer all four years of high school. I also did dance and drama. Academically I took AP, biomedical science, and college courses. I was involved in National Honors Society and the STEP program.

I started my college career in summer 2016. I originally wanted to go for nursing but lost interest and took a year off in fall 2018. I came back in fall of 2019 and decided to switch my major to mass comm. I have to say I'm really happy with where I am at right now. I really enjoy all of my courses, I've especially taken interest in the college radio program.
